FAQs

Common Questions

What skills are you looking for?+
We look for a combination of technical expertise, problem-solving ability, and cultural fit. Specific requirements vary by role but we value passion and willingness to learn above all else.
Is remote work available?+
Yes! Many of our positions offer fully remote options. Some roles may require occasional on-site visits depending on team needs.
What's the interview process like?+
Our process typically includes an initial recruiter call, technical assessment, team interviews, and a final conversation with leadership.
Do you hire junior developers?+
Absolutely! We believe in growing talent. If you're passionate and eager to learn, we'd love to hear from you regardless of experience level.
We foster a collaborative, inclusive environment where everyone's voice matters. We work hard but also have fun doing it!
