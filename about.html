FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Handora Ceylon and our exclusive products.

Why can I only buy from Handora Ceylon directly?
We are the sole manufacturer of our exclusive tea blends. There are no authorized resellers, distributors, or third-party sellers. This ensures that every product you receive is 100% authentic, fresh, and meets our exacting standards. Buying direct also means better prices and guaranteed freshness.
What does "handmade" and "small batch" really mean?
Unlike mass-produced teas, our products involve manual handling at every critical stage — from leaf selection and rolling to blending and packaging. "Small batch" means we produce limited quantities at a time, allowing for meticulous quality control and ensuring each batch maintains its unique character.
Do you offer investment opportunities?
Yes! We offer three tiers of investment partnerships — Bronze ($1,000+), Gold ($5,000+), and Platinum ($25,000+). Each tier offers attractive ROI, exclusive benefits, and varying levels of involvement in our growth journey. Visit our Investment section or contact us for details.
Which countries do you ship to?
We ship worldwide to over 50 countries including USA, UK, EU nations, Australia, Japan, Singapore, UAE, and many more. All shipments are securely packaged and tracked. Express shipping options available for urgent orders.
How should I store my Handora Ceylon tea?
Store in an airtight container away from direct sunlight, heat, moisture, and strong odors. Our premium packaging is designed to maintain freshness, but once opened, consume within 6 months for optimal flavor. Refrigeration is not recommended unless specified for certain herbal varieties.
Are your teas suitable for gifting?
Absolutely! We offer beautiful gift boxes, custom engraving, and corporate gifting solutions. Many of our clients choose Handora Ceylon for VIP gifts, wedding favors, and corporate appreciation packages. Contact us for bespoke gifting options.
