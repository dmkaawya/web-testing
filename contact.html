Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I place an order?

You can order directly through our website's product page, via WhatsApp at +94 70 458 3930, or by emailing us at info@handoraceylon.lk. We accept orders from worldwide and ship to 50+ countries.

What are your payment methods?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and bank transfers for larger orders. For Sri Lankan customers, we also accept cash on delivery.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping times vary by location (5-15 business days). Free shipping is available on orders over $75. All shipments are tracked and insured.

Can I become an investor?

Absolutely! We offer three investment tiers: Bronze ($1,000+), Gold ($5,000+), and Platinum ($25,000+). Each offers attractive ROI and exclusive benefits. Contact us for detailed prospectus.

Are your products really exclusive?

Yes! We are the ONE and ONLY manufacturer of our products. No resellers, no copies exist anywhere in the world. When you buy from Handora Ceylon, you get authentic products directly from the source.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us and we'll arrange a full refund or exchange. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.
